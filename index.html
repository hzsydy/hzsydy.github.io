Short Biography

I am currently a Master of Science in Computer Vision(MSCV) in Carnegie Mellon University. Before that, I earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Automation in Tsinghua University, China.

Since Fall 2016, I have been working at Broadband Network & Digital Media Lab, supervised by Prof. Yebin Liu. I spend a wonderful summer in 2017 in Carnegie Mellon, advised by Prof. Yaser Sheikh.

I worked as a research intern in Megvii Technology Ltd. from Feb 2018 to Jun 2018, supervised by Principle Scientist, Dr. Shuchang Zhou.

My research interests lie in computer vision, robotics and machine learning.

As a boardgame player

    If you could read Chinese and is interested in World War I, see https://hzsydy.gitbook.io/pog/ for the guide.
    We are also constructing an online version of PoG under https://github.com/hzsydy/Paths_of_Glory. Email me if you are interested in.
    Besides wargames, I also play Eurogames like Gaia Project, Food Chain Magnate and Through the Ages.
